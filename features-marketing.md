## 1. Scanning Modes

### Document Scanning (Auto-Detection)

**What it does:**
Intelligent document detection that automatically identifies documents in your camera view, highlights edges in real-time, and captures when perfectly aligned.

**User Benefits:**
- **No manual alignment needed** — AI detects document boundaries instantly
- **Auto-capture** — Just hold steady, the app captures when ready
- **Batch scanning** — Scan multiple pages in one session (up to 100 pages)
- **Perfect perspective correction** — Skewed photos become straight documents
- **Memory-smart processing** — Handles large documents without crashing

**Ideal for:** Contracts, receipts, letters, reports, multi-page documents

---

### ID Card Scanning

**What it does:**
Precision scanning mode with a guide frame matching standard ID card dimensions (ISO/IEC 7810 ID-1). Supports front & back capture with automatic combination.

**User Benefits:**
- **Perfect ID dimensions** — Guide frame ensures nothing is cropped
- **Both-sides support** — Capture front and back in one session
- **Automatic combination** — Front + back combined into single image
- **Privacy-first** — Clear notice that data stays on your device
- **Choose layout** — Vertical (stacked) or horizontal (side-by-side)

**Ideal for:** Driver's licenses, national IDs, credit cards, insurance cards, membership cards

---

### Passport Scanning

**What it does:**
Optimized for international passport data pages with proper aspect ratio (ISO/IEC 7810 ID-3) and fold line indicator for perfect alignment.

**User Benefits:**
- **Passport-optimized dimensions** — Captures entire data page correctly
- **Fold line guide** — Helps align the passport spine
- **Both pages support** — Capture multiple passport pages
- **Privacy protection** — All processing happens locally on device
- **Travel-ready** — Perfect for visa applications, travel documentation

**Ideal for:** International passports, travel visas, travel documentation

---

### Business Card Scanning

**What it does:**
Scans business cards and automatically extracts contact information using advanced OCR, then saves directly to your iPhone Contacts.

**User Benefits:**
- **Instant contact extraction** — Names, phones, emails extracted automatically
- **Save to Contacts** — One-tap save to your iPhone address book
- **Smart deduplication** — Combines info from both sides of card
- **Social media detection** — Finds LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram handles
- **Editable before saving** — Review and correct before adding to contacts

**Extracted Information:**
- Full name with proper formatting
- Company/Organization
- Job title/Position
- Multiple email addresses
- Multiple phone numbers (mobile, work, fax)
- Website URLs
- Social media profiles
- Street address

**Ideal for:** Networking events, conferences, client meetings, trade shows

---

### Book Scanning

**What it does:**
Landscape-oriented scanning mode for open books with automatic page splitting. The orange spine line helps you align the book's center, then each capture automatically becomes two separate pages.

**User Benefits:**
- **Scan 2 pages at once** — One photo becomes two document pages
- **Spine alignment guide** — Orange line shows exactly where to place book center
- **Automatic splitting** — AI separates left and right pages perfectly
- **Preserve reading order** — Left page first, then right page
- **Faster digitization** — Half the time to scan entire books

**Ideal for:** Textbooks, reference books, journals, magazines, photo albums, old records

## 8. Security & Privacy

### App Lock

**What it does:**
Lock the entire app behind biometric authentication or passcode.

**Authentication Options:**
- **Face ID** — Face recognition (iPhone X and later)
- **Touch ID** — Fingerprint (older devices with Touch ID)
- **Passcode** — 4-digit PIN backup

**User Benefits:**
- **Complete privacy** — Nobody can open your documents
- **Biometric convenience** — Just look or touch to unlock
- **Passcode fallback** — Works if biometric fails
- **Configurable timeout** — Set lock delay after leaving app

### Secure Storage

**What it does:**
All sensitive data (passcodes, OAuth tokens, keys) stored in iOS Keychain with hardware encryption.

**User Benefits:**
- **Hardware security** — Encrypted by device Secure Enclave
- **Survives reinstall** — Some data persists for account recovery
- **No plain text** — Passwords hashed with SHA256
- **Cross-app isolation** — Only ScanLens can access

**User Benefits:**
