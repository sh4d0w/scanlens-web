The Document Scanner Built for iPhone

Native iOS app with deep system integration. iCloud sync, Shortcuts automation, and camera optimizations that only a true iPhone app can deliver.

Why a Native iPhone App Matters

ScanLens isn't a cross-platform app ported to iOS—it's built from the ground up specifically for iPhone using Swift and Apple's native frameworks. This means faster performance, lower battery usage, and seamless integration with the features you already use on your iPhone.

Native apps can access iPhone capabilities that web apps and cross-platform tools simply cannot match. From the camera API to system-level integrations like Shortcuts and Siri, ScanLens takes full advantage of what makes iPhone special.

The result is an app that feels like it belongs on your iPhone. The interface follows Apple's Human Interface Guidelines, animations are smooth at 120fps on ProMotion displays, and everything just works the way you expect it to.

Seamless iCloud Sync

Your scanned documents follow you everywhere thanks to deep iCloud integration. Scan a receipt on your iPhone at a restaurant, and it's already waiting on your Mac when you get home to do expenses. No manual syncing, no third-party cloud accounts required.

How iCloud Sync Works

ScanLens stores your documents in iCloud Drive, making them accessible from any device signed into your Apple ID. Changes sync automatically in the background—rename a file on your iPad, and the change appears on your iPhone within seconds.

  • Automatic backup: Your scans are safely stored in iCloud, protected by Apple's encryption
  • Offline access: Downloaded documents remain available even without internet
  • Storage management: Optimize storage by keeping full-resolution files in the cloud
  • Cross-device handoff: Start scanning on iPhone, continue organizing on iPad or Mac

Works with iCloud Family Sharing

Share scanned documents with family members through iCloud shared folders. Perfect for household paperwork, school documents, or family projects.

Automate Everything with Shortcuts

The iOS Shortcuts app transforms ScanLens into a powerful automation tool. Create custom workflows that scan, process, and file documents exactly the way you want—without touching any buttons.

Example Automations

  • Receipt scanner: Tap one shortcut to scan a receipt, add today's date to the filename, and save to your Expenses folder
  • Contract workflow: Scan a document, add your signature, convert to PDF/A, and email to your lawyer
  • Time-based filing: Automatically organize scans into folders by month or project
  • NFC triggers: Tap an NFC tag at your desk to instantly open ScanLens in scanning mode

You can also trigger ScanLens actions from other apps using Shortcuts. Scan documents directly from your task manager, note-taking app, or any app that supports the Shortcuts framework.

Optimized for iPhone Camera

ScanLens is tuned for every iPhone camera system, from the iPhone SE to the latest Pro models. Our algorithms adapt to your specific device, ensuring optimal results regardless of which iPhone you use.

Camera Features We Support

  • Wide and ultra-wide lenses: Scan large documents or whiteboards with the ultra-wide lens without distortion
  • Telephoto scanning: On Pro models, use the telephoto lens to scan distant whiteboards or posters
  • ProRAW capture: For maximum quality on supported devices, capture in ProRAW before processing
  • Night mode: Scan in low-light conditions with Night mode support for clearer results
  • Optical image stabilization: Take advantage of OIS for sharper handheld scans

Smart Flash Control

ScanLens intelligently controls the flash to avoid glare on glossy documents. Multiple exposures are automatically blended to eliminate hotspots while maintaining even illumination.

Privacy Built Into the Foundation

Apple users care about privacy, and so do we. ScanLens processes all documents locally on your device—your scans never pass through our servers. This isn't just a feature; it's a fundamental design principle.

How We Protect Your Data

  • On-device OCR: Text recognition happens entirely on your iPhone using Apple's Vision framework
  • No cloud processing: Unlike competitors, we don't upload your documents for "enhancement"
  • No analytics on content: We never analyze, read, or index your scanned documents
  • iCloud encryption: Documents synced to iCloud are encrypted end-to-end

Check our App Privacy label on the App Store—ScanLens collects no data linked to your identity. Your documents are yours alone.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does ScanLens sync with iCloud?

Yes, ScanLens fully integrates with iCloud Drive. Your scanned documents automatically sync across all your Apple devices—iPhone, iPad, and Mac—so you can access them anywhere. Sync happens in the background without any manual steps required.

Can I use ScanLens with Shortcuts app?

Yes, ScanLens supports iOS Shortcuts for powerful automation. You can create custom workflows to automatically scan, name, and file documents to specific folders. Combine with other Shortcuts actions to build complex workflows like scanning, signing, and emailing contracts in one tap.

What iOS version does ScanLens require?

ScanLens requires iOS 15.0 or later. We always support the latest iOS features while maintaining compatibility with older devices. For the best experience, we recommend keeping your iPhone updated to the latest iOS version.

Does ScanLens work with all iPhone cameras?

ScanLens is optimized for every iPhone camera system, from iPhone SE to the latest Pro models. We support wide, ultra-wide, and telephoto lenses, plus advanced features like ProRAW capture, Night mode, and optical image stabilization on compatible devices.

Is ScanLens better than the built-in Notes scanner?

The Notes scanner handles basic scanning, but ScanLens offers advanced features professionals need: OCR in 50+ languages, digital signatures, PDF/A archival format, compression options, password protection, and advanced organization tools. If you scan more than occasionally, ScanLens is worth it.
